## 8. Player Progression

### 8.1 Player XP
- Player XP = **aggregate XP of all rings currently in the player's possession**
- Rings earn XP through use in battle
- Losing a ring through staking permanently reduces player XP
- Winning a staked ring from an opponent permanently increases player XP

### 8.2 Spirit Gauge Growth

The **spirit gauge maximum** is the primary expression of player progression (see §12). As aggregate ring XP grows, the spirit gauge maximum increases — unlocking higher carry capacity and more recharges per sleep cycle.

- More spirit → carry more rings on expedition (higher carry cap)
- More spirit → restore more ring uses per sleep (deeper field endurance)
- XP thresholds for spirit increases: **TBD during tuning**

The spirit gauge replaces the old "inventory expansion" mechanic as the progression axis — growth feels earned through combat rather than purchased arbitrarily.

### 8.3 World Access Gating
- Higher player XP (and therefore spirit) unlocks new areas of the overworld
- Losing significant XP through staking can **revoke access** to areas previously unlocked
- This creates genuine long-term stakes beyond any individual duel

### 8.4 Full Inventory (at camp)
- All rings the player has ever acquired are stored at camp
- No hard cap on total inventory — the camp holds everything
- Carry cap (how many you take on expedition) is the meaningful limit, not total ownership
- Garments and articles from merchants can further expand carry cap beyond the spirit-derived default
