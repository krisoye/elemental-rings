### Changed

- Charge orb now starts at 0° (aimed at opponent) and swings outward to the first
  extreme over a 250ms arm leg, instead of starting at −45° and sweeping immediately.
  The sweet-spot crossing moves from ~600ms to ~850ms on the first post-arm pass.
- Added tap grace window (150–249ms): a hold inside `[CHARGE_THRESHOLD_MS, CHARGE_ARM_MS)`
  resolves as a normal tap hit rather than a charged miss, preventing accidental misses
  on very short charged holds.
- Charge miss orb now flies toward the opponent (derived from `Math.sign(enemyX − from.x)`)
  instead of always flying off to the right.
