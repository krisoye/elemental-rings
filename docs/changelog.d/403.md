### Bug Fixes
- **Snow biome bridges walkable**: Fixed bridge tiles from `ts_snow` in the `behind` layer being impassable. `SnowScene` no longer overrides `tileLayerCollisionMode` to `'non-empty'`; `forest_snow_gate` carves out of `ForestScene`'s `non-empty` override and marks its obstacle tiles (`berry_and_trees`, `terrain_plains_fantasy`) with `collides: true` so `'property'` mode blocks walls/trees while leaving bridge planks walkable.
